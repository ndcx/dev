Why ND Construction Pvt Ltd

A renovation should not be a leap of faith.

Renovations are notorious for delays, scope creep, and surprise bills. Here's how we make sure your project is none of those things.

Written scope, fixed price

Before we start, you receive a written scope of work and an itemized price. The price you agree to is the price you pay — no surprise additions halfway through.

Fixed delivery date

The completion date is written into the contract. We sign a timeline commitment, and we hit it. If we're late, you'll know why — and we'll commit to a new date in writing.

In-house teams, not subcontractors

Our masons, plumbers, electricians, painters and tilers are full-time ND Construction employees. You don't get one contractor on Monday and a different one on Tuesday.

Engineer for complex work

If your renovation involves structural changes, load-bearing wall removal, or significant plumbing/electrical rework, a qualified engineer assesses the work before it starts.

Written warranty

Bathroom waterproofing carries a 5-year warranty. Other renovation work carries its own documented warranty terms. You receive the warranty at handover.

Photo updates on progress

You receive photo updates of work in progress at key milestones. You always know what's been done, what's happening today, and what's next — without having to be on site.

About the Company

A construction company built like a structure — on a foundation of trust.

ND Construction Pvt Ltd was founded in 2010 by Babu Deyoan in Burdwan with a single belief: building a home is one of the most important decisions a family will ever make, and it deserves more than a phone number and a quote.

From our early days in Burdwan, ND Construction has grown into a professionally managed construction company — qualified engineers, in-house finishing teams, written milestone contracts, and a 5-year structural warranty. Every system we use today reflects the standards our clients trust us for.

Today, families across Burdwan, Goda, Memari, Kalna, Katwa, Galsi, Ausgram and Durgapur trust ND Construction with new builds, renovations and finishing work because we treat their project the way a company should — with engineering rigor, transparent contracts, and a team that shows up every day until the keys are handed over.

How We De-Risk Your Build

Engineering. Inspections. Warranty.

A construction project has too many ways to go wrong. We've built the systems that make sure it doesn't.

01

Engineer on every project

A highly qualified civil/structural engineer supervises every ND Construction project — from soil assessment and foundation design to final structural inspection. Drawings are reviewed, not assumed.

02

Material & milestone inspections

Materials are checked on arrival. Each construction stage — foundation, lintel, roof, plastering, finishing — passes a defined inspection before the next stage begins. Nothing is hidden behind the next layer of work.

03

5-year structural warranty

Every ND Construction build carries a 5-year structural warranty. If anything we built moves, cracks, or fails for reasons within our scope of work, we come back and fix it — at no cost to you.

04

Weekly progress with photos

You receive a written weekly progress update with photographs from the site. You always know what's been done, what's next, and whether we're on schedule — without having to visit the site yourself.

05

In-house teams, not subcontractors

Our masons, painters, plumbers, electricians and tilers are full-time employees. They are trained on our methods, accountable to our project managers, and insured under our company.

06

Written milestone contracts

Every project runs on a written agreement with payment tied to construction milestones. You pay for completed work, not promises. The scope, the cost, the timeline — all in writing before the first brick is laid.

How We Work

A project, in six predictable steps.

From your first call to the day we hand over the keys, here is what working with ND Construction Pvt Ltd actually looks like.

01

Consultation

Call, WhatsApp, or fill the form. Tell us about your site, your requirements, your timeline. We respond within one business day.

02

Site visit with our engineer

A qualified engineer visits your site at no cost. We assess the soil, the existing structure, the access — and discuss what is feasible within your budget.

03

Itemized estimate

You receive a written estimate broken into materials, labour, and timeline. Every line item, every quantity, every rate — in writing. No verbal numbers.

04

Contract & milestones

Once approved, we sign a written agreement with payment tied to construction milestones. You pay for completed work — never for promises.

05

Execution & weekly reports

Our in-house team executes the work. You receive a written progress update with photographs every week. Engineer inspections happen at each milestone.

06

Handover & warranty

On completion, we walk the site with you, address a snag list if anything needs touching up, and hand over the warranty documentation. The relationship doesn't end at handover.

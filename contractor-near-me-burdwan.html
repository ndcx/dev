Why ND Construction Pvt Ltd

What makes ND Construction a reputed name

Over 15 years of delivering engineer-supervised projects, written milestone contracts, and in-house quality — that's what sets us apart from ordinary contractors.

Engineer on every project

A highly qualified civil/structural engineer reviews your drawings, supervises execution, and signs off on every milestone. The technical authority is real — not a name on paper.

5-year structural warranty

Every ND Construction build carries a 5-year structural warranty. If anything we built fails within our scope, we come back and fix it — at no cost to you.

Written milestone contracts

You sign a written agreement with payment tied to construction milestones. You pay for completed work — never for promises. Scope, cost, timeline — all in writing.

In-house teams, not subcontractors

Masons, painters, plumbers, electricians and tilers are full-time ND Construction employees. Trained on our methods, accountable to our project managers.

Itemized estimates

Every estimate is itemized into materials, labour, and timeline. You see what every rupee pays for — and you can verify the quantities, rates, and scope before signing.

Weekly written progress

You receive a written progress update with photographs from the site every week. You always know what's been done, what's next, and whether we're on schedule.

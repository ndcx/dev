What's Included

A complete home build, managed as one project.

Foundation, structure, masonry, plastering, electrical, plumbing, flooring, painting — managed by a single project team with one engineer and one contract.

RCC Frame Structure

Engineer-designed reinforced cement concrete frame with proper seismic compliance. The skeleton of your home, built to last generations. We do not use load-bearing for multi-storey builds.

Brick Masonry

Quality brick masonry using proper interlocking, mortar ratios and curing. We use trusted local brick sources, with material inspections on every delivery.

Plastering & Surface Prep

Internal and external plastering with proper thickness, curing, and surface preparation. The layer that determines how paint and finish look — and how long they last.

Electrical & Plumbing

In-house electricians and plumbers handle wiring, fixtures, drainage, and water lines. Coordinated with the build schedule so nothing is rushed or patched later.

Painting & Finishing

Interior and exterior painting with our trained finishing team. Premium putty, primer, and paint application — no shortcuts, no skipped coats.

Doors, Windows & Fixtures

Doors, windows, grills, hardware, sanitary fittings and electrical fixtures — all specified in the written estimate, with itemized costs so there are no surprises.

Why ND Construction Pvt Ltd

What makes ND Construction a reputed name

Over 15 years of delivering engineer-supervised projects, written milestone contracts, and in-house quality — that's what sets us apart from ordinary contractors.

Engineer on every project

A highly qualified civil/structural engineer reviews your drawings, supervises execution, and signs off on every milestone. The technical authority is real — not a name on paper.

5-year structural warranty

Every ND Construction build carries a 5-year structural warranty. If anything we built fails within our scope, we come back and fix it — at no cost to you.

Written milestone contracts

You sign a written agreement with payment tied to construction milestones. You pay for completed work — never for promises. Scope, cost, timeline — all in writing before the first brick is laid.

In-house teams, not subcontractors

Masons, painters, plumbers, electricians and tilers are full-time ND Construction employees. Trained on our methods, accountable to our project managers, insured under our company.

Itemized estimates

Every estimate is itemized into materials, labour, and timeline. You see what every rupee pays for — and you can verify the quantities, rates, and scope before signing.

Weekly written progress

You receive a written progress update with photographs from the site every week. You always know what's been done, what's next, and whether we're on schedule.
